Our Services

Custom Solutions for Your Thought Into Wearable

Embroidery

Embroidery stitches designs onto fabric with durable threads, adding a professional and personalized touch to apparel and accessories.

Digitizing

Digitizing converts artwork into a digital format that machines use to stitch precise and detailed embroidery designs.

Vector Art

Vector Art transforms artwork into scalable digital formats, ensuring crisp, clear images for high-quality printing and embroidery.

Custom Patches

Patches embroidery creates custom designs on fabric patches for easy application and versatile branding.

Custom apparel features personalized designs and details on clothing, tailored to reflect your unique style or brand.
